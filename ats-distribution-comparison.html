Care & Bloom recruiting research

ATS vs Job Distribution

A readable decision surface for choosing whether to buy a multiposter, use an ATS, or automate parts of job-board distribution ourselves.

Recommendation

Start distribution-first. Do not replace the ATS workflow yet.

1. Pilot JobTarget or eQuestXpress

Best fit for the real pain: one post to many boards while keeping Care & Bloom's application form and Notion intake.

2. Prepare the current site

Add source tracking, real resume file/URL handling, Google Jobs schema, and a JSON/XML jobs feed.

3. Compare ATS only as fallback

Use JazzHR, Workable, and Ashby if the multiposters cannot preserve field parity or source tracking.

Operating assumptions

10-20active roles per month
100-300+applicants per role
< $1Ksoftware budget, before paid board spend
Hybridnative apply only if required fields arrive
Chosen path

Distribution-first maps to the actual problem

The current workflow already has a custom application form and Notion intake. The gap is posting reach, source tracking, and board coverage. JobTarget and eQuest are built for that exact top-of-funnel problem.

Fallback path

ATS only if it earns the workflow

JazzHR, Workable, and Ashby matter if hosted forms, native apply, automations, scorecards, or bulk review become easier than the existing Notion workflow.

DIY helper

DIY improves readiness, not full distribution

Google Jobs schema, source URLs, a jobs feed, and resume storage should happen anyway. Direct LinkedIn/Indeed/ZipRecruiter posting is partner-gated enough that DIY cannot reliably replace a distributor.

Do not lead here

Enterprise ATSs are misaligned for now

Greenhouse, Lever, and SmartRecruiters are strong products, but their center of gravity is full recruiting systems, not low-friction multiposting under $1K/month.

DIY-friendly

Google Jobs

Add JobPosting JSON-LD to public role pages. This is the easiest direct improvement to make without a vendor.

Approval-sensitive

Indeed

XML/feed and Indeed Apply exist, but eligibility, quality checks, sponsorship rules, and moderation can constrain exposure.

Partner-gated

LinkedIn

Job Posting API is for authorized third parties, ATSs, and distributors. Practical automation usually flows through approved partners.

Partner-oriented

ZipRecruiter

Partner job APIs and apply webhooks exist, but a distributor or ATS partner is the realistic route.

Unresolved board coverage

Public docs did not conclusively prove support for OnlineJobs.ph, Kalibrr, Bossjob, Glints, or JobStreet across the shortlist. Treat these as high-priority demo questions, especially for Philippines/Asia hiring.

Capture source

Store source, vendor/campaign, and referrer in Notion from URLs like ?source=linkedin&utm_vendor=jobtarget.

2

Handle resumes

Current form stores filenames only. Add upload storage or a resume URL field before external distribution.

Public role pages should include structured JobPosting JSON-LD for direct Google Jobs eligibility.

4

Build JSON/XML feed with title, slug, description, location/remote, role URL, apply URL, and close date.
