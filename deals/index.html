The five shapes
Build We take a category thesis from spec to shelf: product development in Shenzhen, brand and funnel built in-house, launched on our own engine.
Buy We acquire brands with proof and give them what they were missing: manufacturing control, creative volume, and a growth team that owns the number.
Fund We back operators with product taste and customer demand, and stay useful after the wire: sourcing, creative, distribution, and category fluency.
Partner You keep the company. We plug specific stations into it: manufacturing, launch creative, growth, or Asia distribution, priced by what moves.
Co-own Shared upside on shared work: we build or scale together and split ownership by contribution, not by template.
