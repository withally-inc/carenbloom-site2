Working brief

Care & Bloom & Friends 3-Day Scale Sprint

June 19-21 9AM-7PM HKT Hong Kong + Remote Working doc

We are thinking about a small group of operators in Hong Kong, plus a remote crew, working for three focused days on specific commercial goals. The goal is simple: revenue/day at a workable ROAS.

Core idea

Focused work. Sharp people. Clear numbers.

Every team comes in with a number. A real brand or project, a target revenue/day, a target ROAS, a defined bottleneck, and the support needed to move fast.

Sprint flow

How do we make success inevitable?

  1. 1:1 calls with each participant or team.
  2. Strategy session per brand before work begins.
  3. Research and funnel references completed before arrival.
  4. Remote crew lined up around specific workstreams.
  5. Three days of focused work, unblockers, and insight sharing.
  6. Final shareout around revenue/day, ROAS, and next moves.

Team shape

Each workstream needs coverage.

Lead Point operator.

One person owns the target, decisions, blockers, and final shareout.

Creative Creative strategist.

Owns the angle, copy, offer framing, and creative direction around the target.

Build Page builders.

Pages, advertorials, quizzes, checkout flows, and iteration speed.

Launch Uploaders / media buyers.

People who can get campaigns live and keep the feedback loop moving.

Production Creative production.

Static ads, videos, edits, variants, and fast asset production for testing.

Remote crew Tied to outcomes.

Remote help should attach to specific workstreams, not float vaguely.

Rundown

Focused work, sharing, and co-learning.

01 / Focused work
Execute against the number.

Teams spend the majority of each day building, launching, reviewing, and unblocking against revenue/day and ROAS.

02 / Sharing
Short, tactical sessions.

Operator insight only when it helps the work: ads, offer, page, creative, build, ops, or measurement.

03 / Co-learning
Make the room smarter in real time.

People compare what they are seeing, unblock each other, and pressure-test the next move around actual tests.
