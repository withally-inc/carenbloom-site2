[01] Why

Three days. One number.

Most people do not need another vague growth conversation. They need a serious target, uninterrupted work, sharp operators around them, and fast feedback on the thing that matters: how do we get this offer to produce revenue at an acceptable ROAS?

[02] Who

People who bring signal.

Participant profile Operators with a revenue target.

You should know what you are trying to hit per day, what ROAS makes it work, and where the current bottleneck is.

Room standard People who share insight.

The room works if everyone brings what they know: ads, funnel, offer, creative, operations, retention, or sales.

Not for Passive networking.

This is not a meetup. Come to work, ask for help, and show the commercial movement by the end.

[03] What

Work blocks plus insight sharing.

The sprint mixes focused working sessions with short, tactical insight sharing from speakers and operators. The content is in service of the work: ROAS, offer, page, creative, spend, conversion, and revenue.

[05] Commitment

No vague goals.

“We plan to hit HK$X/day at Yx ROAS.”

That is the bar. Everything else is supporting work: ads, funnel/page, creative, offer, analytics, ops, and support.

Prep

  • Lock revenue/day target and ROAS target.
  • Identify the current bottleneck.
  • Line up remote editors and funnel/page builders.

Sprint

  • Focused working blocks.
  • Insight sharing from speakers and operators.
  • Meetings and support around the highest-leverage constraint.

Shareout

  • What revenue/day are we closer to?
  • What ROAS did we prove or disprove?
  • What needs to happen next?

[06] Rules / Vibes

Rules / vibes.

01 / Number Come with a number.

“I plan to hit revenue of $X/day at Y ROAS.”

02 / Mindset No casual trying.

Ask what has to be true for this to work. Then go after that seriously.

03 / Prep Prep before matters.

At least 1 call before, probably 2, to get clear on goals, blockers, and support needed.

04 / Focus Focus during the sprint.

The 3 days are for actual sprinting: deep work, quick unblockers, useful sharing.

05 / Share Share what you know.

Bring insight from ads, offer, funnel, creative, sales, ops, retention, or whatever you are strong at.

06 / Support Set up support beforehand.

We can help line up editors, funnel/page help, or remote support. You should also set up your own support where needed.

Before June 19

Prep creates the sprint.

  • Participant goals need to be sharpened before arrival.
  • Remote crew should be assigned to specific revenue targets, not vague tasks.
  • Pages, funnels, ad accounts, analytics, assets, and decision-makers should be ready before the first work block.
  • The WhatsApp group should coordinate goals, blockers, support slots, and daily shareouts.
