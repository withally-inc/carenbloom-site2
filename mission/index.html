Mission [01]

Build where trust changes behavior.

We focus on health categories where people need more than content or commerce. They need products they can believe in, advice they can use, and brands that respect the stakes.

Four areas

One standard. Four categories.

01

Sexual health

Care for intimacy, hormones, fertility, pleasure, and agency still asks people to navigate stigma. We build brands that make the category easier to enter and safer to trust.

02

Longevity

Longevity is moving from abstract promises to practical healthspan: energy, recovery, prevention, metabolic health, and routines people can keep.

03

Mental health

People need support that feels accessible and human, with enough clinical seriousness to be trusted and enough cultural fluency to be used.

04

Education

Better health decisions start with language people understand. Education turns expert knowledge into routines, confidence, and behavior change.
