How we work together

Focused teams. Player coaches.

01

Pilot and co-pilot teams

Small pods own specific KPIs with a clear pilot, a strong co-pilot, a visible scorecard, and enough autonomy to move within the day.

02

Player coaches

Senior owners coach and play: they build the system, mentor the team, and personally take the hardest position when an area is new, broken, or below floor.

What player-coach means

Coach: what you own

  • The KPI your team is hitting. The daily report, the Tuesday call, and the number.
  • The frameworks, scorecards, and rituals. The operating system everyone runs on.
  • Your people have what they need to ship. Resourcing, unblocking, and escalation before momentum dies.

Player: what you do not delegate

  • Below floor, fix it yourself today. If the work is broken, jump in.
  • The new and evolving areas. Own the work before the recipe is locked.
  • Rewriting structure as gaps appear. Fight silos and work that does not ladder to the number.
