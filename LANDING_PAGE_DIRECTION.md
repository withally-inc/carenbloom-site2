# Care & Bloom Landing Page Direction

## Source Reference

Elevator Goods is the structural reference, not the visual identity.

Borrow:
- Giant central index.
- Four or five dominant words as navigation.
- Sparse surrounding instruments.
- Confident low-copy posture.
- Full-screen first impression.

Change:
- More color.
- More AI-native operating signals.
- More physical moat language.
- More Asia/platform specificity.
- White Care & Bloom logo only.

## Core Index

```text
MISSION [01]
BRANDS  [02]
CAPITAL [03]
TALENTS [04]
```

## Section Roles

### Mission

Worldview first. Care & Bloom believes digital tooling is becoming cheap and abundant, while physical moats still compound: manufacturing, regulatory fluency, trust, distribution, community, and regional depth.

### Brands

The most colorful section. Show the categories, brand-building appetite, launch velocity, and willingness to experiment. This is where the page should feel alive.

### Capital

The institutional section. Explain why the platform can build, buy, fund, and scale brands better because it has operating infrastructure, AI leverage, and physical relationships.

### Talents

The human network. Experts, celebrities, creators, operators, founders, advisors, and category faces are not endorsements pasted on top. They are part of the ownership and trust engine.

## Hero Behavior

The first viewport should be an index, not a pitch deck.

- White logo visible.
- Four huge words dominate.
- Small manifesto copy sits at one edge.
- A small live instrument sits at another edge.
- Hovering each word reveals its proof phrase and section color.
- Mobile keeps the same four-word stack with fewer instruments.

## Suggested Proof Phrases

- **MISSION:** Physical moats outlast cheap bits.
- **BRANDS:** Color, category, community, repeat.
- **CAPITAL:** Platform leverage for health empires.
- **TALENTS:** Experts and icons become owners.

## Visual System

Base: mineral, muted, investor-grade.

Interruptions: saturated coral, cobalt, lime, yellow, magenta.

Motion: slow live signals, sharp hover response, scroll-state color shifts. No bouncy motion. No decoration without meaning.

## Implementation Notes

- Use `DESIGN.md` as token and rule source.
- Keep cards rare. This page is an index and operating system, not a tiled deck.
- Use semantic sections and keyboard-focusable index links.
- Support `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Avoid all current-site and DTC-deck styling except the logo asset.
